The keyboard mappings:

X for running

T for fireballs

Arrows for up/down/right/left

H for replay


The gamepad mappings:

A for jump

B for running

X for fireballs

left/right/down on joystick for movement
R to reset level
Q to quit


Notes: The level will reset upon mario dying to an enemy. Enemies will die if they fall of the level. 
Also, the first time running may take a little while to load, as all of the sound files were large even after compression.
